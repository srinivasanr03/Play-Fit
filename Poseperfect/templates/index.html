Instructions for Warrior Pose

To perform the Warrior Pose (Virabhadrasana) correctly:

  1. Stand with your feet about 3-4 feet apart, with your right foot facing forward and your left foot turned slightly inward.
  2. Raise your arms parallel to the floor, with your palms facing down.
  3. Bend your right knee over your right ankle, making sure your thigh is parallel to the floor.
  4. Keep your left leg straight and grounded, pressing firmly into the mat.
  5. Turn your head to look over your right hand and hold the pose for 20-30 seconds.
  6. Release the pose and repeat on the opposite side.

Ensure to keep your torso upright, avoid leaning forward, and focus on your breath while holding the pose.
